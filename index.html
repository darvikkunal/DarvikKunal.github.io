What I Do

Extract Transform and Load (ETL)

I will build ETL pipelines to extract data from various sources, transform it into a consistent format, and load it into a data warehouse or data lake. This process includes gathering data from databases, APIs, or files, cleaning and converting it, and transferring it for easy access and analysis. This ensures the data is prepared and stored optimally.

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

I will describe the data using statistical and visualization techniques to highlight important aspects, facilitating further analysis. I will identify general patterns in the data, such as outliers and unexpected features. creating various graphs and plots to identify key patterns in data.

Data quality evaluation

I will thoroughly assess data quality and test assumptions by checking for missing values, identifying inconsistencies, and ensuring data integrity. Additionally, I will evaluate the data distribution to confirm it meets the necessary assumptions for further analysis. These assessments will ensure the data's reliability and validity.
